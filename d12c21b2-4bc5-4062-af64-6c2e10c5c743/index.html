Pau Masó

Pau Masó was born in Girona, Catalonia, Spain as Pau Masó Coromina on January 14th, 1986. He is an actor, writer, director and producer. Known as a "self-made man", he is hard working, dedicated and persevering. To date, Pau has appeared in different productions, including "Aleksandr's Price" (2013), and "The Art of Losing" (2015).
